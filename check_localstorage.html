LocalStorage Debug

Open this file in your browser at http://localhost:3000 then open the browser console to see your localStorage contents.

Or run this in your browser console while on the application:

console.log('Token:', localStorage.getItem('token'));
console.log('User:', JSON.parse(localStorage.getItem('user') || '{}'));

Fix Instructions:

  1. Open your browser at http://localhost:3000
  2. Click "Logout" if you're logged in
  3. Log in again with your credentials (gvemula@mail.yu.edu)
  4. The property should now be assigned correctly

The issue is that your current session has old user data cached. Logging out and logging back in will refresh this data with the correct property_id.
